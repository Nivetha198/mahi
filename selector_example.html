Alagar Public School – Promoted by Alagar charitable Foundation, is an initiative of the eight decade old business conglomerate, Alagar Group of Companies, Tuticorin. We, at Alagar Public School, firmly believe in the ancient Indian traditional education system where teachers are to be venerated and who are dedicated to help us to know ourselves and understand our innate mind…
